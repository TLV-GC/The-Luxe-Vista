📧 Gmail Email Notifications via EmailJS
Follow these steps to make every customer in-site message automatically arrive in your Gmail inbox (theluxevista2@gmail.com). This is completely free for up to 200 emails per month.
1
Create a free EmailJS account — Go to emailjs.com and sign up for free.
2
Add Gmail as an email service — In your EmailJS dashboard, go to Email Services → Add New Service → Gmail. Connect your theluxevista2@gmail.com account.
3
Create an email template — Go to Email Templates → Create New. Use variables like {{from_name}}, {{phone}}, {{product}}, {{message}}. Set the To Email to your Gmail address.
4
Copy your credentials — From your EmailJS account, copy your Public Key, Service ID, and Template ID and paste them below.
5
Save and add the SDK to your site — After saving below, add the EmailJS SDK to your index.html (instructions appear after saving).
